Band Bio

tiny specks are a dynamic indie/garage rock trio of songwriters that use clever lyrics and intricate guitar layering to take you to another world. Drawing inspiration from bands like Bikini Kill, The B-52's, and Sonic Youth, tiny specks delivers a powerful live performance that makes a crowd move and shout along. Get ready to be swept away by their infectious energy. Go on a unique musical journey with tiny specks!
